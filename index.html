Leap

The Free Linux Distro

Leap is a free Linux distro that focuses on simplicity and the ability to do more

#It has

--The Budgie Desktop Environment(Dependant on the variant of Leap you get)

--Wine so you can run all of your favourite apps.

--The Raven Sidebar to do things more fluently

--Blender for the occasional animation attempts

--Inkscape for all your drawing needs

--LXTerminal and PCManFM File Manager preinstalled

--The simplicity of the Budgie Menu

--Different desktop environments available

--The complete LibreOffice Collection

--Docky Preinstalled

--Testdrive Available
